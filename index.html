About Me

I am a Research Scientist at the Scientific and Industrial Research and Development Centre (SIRDC), Zimbabwe, specializing in Data Science, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Software Engineering. My work focuses on turning complex data into actionable insights that improve decision-making, operational efficiency, and innovation across sectors.

I have deep experience in predictive modeling, AI-driven automation, deep learning, and advanced analytics, including classification, regression, clustering, NLP, and neural networks. I design, deploy, and integrate intelligent systems into production environments with a focus on scalability and real-world impact.

My technical range spans Python, R, C#, Java, and JavaScript, full-stack development with Spring Boot, Django and React, big data processing with Spark and Hadoop, and cloud platforms including AWS and Microsoft Azure. I also deliver capacity-building programs and technical training across data science, AI, and analytics, with a strong commitment to ethical and responsible AI.
